Ministry of Thought is a structured companion to the video content.

The main purpose of the site is to hold supporting pages for videos: references, clarifications, corrections, related topics, and curated pathways through the material.

It is not intended to be a busy blog or news feed. The emphasis is on helping visitors move from one meaningful idea to the next.

## What you will find here

- supporting pages for videos
- references and further reading
- topic pages that gather related videos together
- series pages for curated viewing paths
- corrections or updates where needed

## Where to start

You can begin with the **Topics** directory, a **Series** page, or the most **Recent** videos.
